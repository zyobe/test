Our campus is set within 230 acres of beautiful Cotswold countryside, located between Cheltenham and Cirencester

As parents and teachers, the most precious thing we can do is give children the freedom to learn and develop and enjoy the adventure of growing up. At Rendcomb College, pupils are free to be themselves and free to take on big challenges because they know that to FAIL is only the First Attempt In Learning.

Our pupils are free to explore academically or literally on a 230 acre site with a Forest School, a Wilderness and with many outdoor activities and leadership opportunities. Pupils are free to build lasting friendships in a community that nurtures individuality within the safe-keeping of the Rendcomb Family.

We have three main entry points at Year 7, Year 9 and Year 12. Entry into other year groups is also possible dependent on availability. We recognise that family circumstances and commitments don't always fit into the school year and we do welcome applications throughout the year.

It is important that you make the right choice for your family and the Admissions Team is here to help you do just that.
